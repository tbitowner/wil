Welcome to Willard Wetterland Architects, PLLC

Willard Wetterland Architects, PLLC, is a multi-disciplined design and service-oriented architectural firm located in Scottsdale, Arizona. We offer a complete architectural service.

Our professional objective is to create an aesthetically pleasing presentation of the design solution, through the comprehensive, efficient, innovative, and cost-effective administration of the construction disciplines.

We achieve this through the development of clear, complete, concise, user-friendly construction documents.

With over thirty years of experience, including work on several national and local award-winning projects, Wil has compiled an impressive list of highest-quality buildings, constructed throughout the Northeast and Southwest regions of the United States. Past projects include, but are not limited to, public and municipal projects, corporate, retail, educational, office, commercial, industrial, institutional, medical, housing (low-income and development), banking, interiors, custom residential, religious, and historic renovation.

Design Philosophy
Willard Wetterland Architects operates as a teaching studio, where creative dialogue and expression of individual and collective ideas are encouraged as integral to the process of project production. We strive to express ourselves in building form, with the highest quality design, supported by technical expertise and precise attention to detail. An artistically inspired finished product, delivered on time and within budget, is the ultimate objective.
